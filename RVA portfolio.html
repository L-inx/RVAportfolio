Artist Statement

My practice explores everyday objects and lived environments through accessible visual formats sketches, found material collage, pattern, photography, and digital design. I focus on clear communication: simplifying forms so they convey mood and narrative quickly. I choose subjects that balance the mundane with moments of quiet intensity a cluttered desk, a motorcycle at dusk, or a hand cut collage and treat each as an opportunity to study light, rhythm, and composition. Methodologically, I value iteration: small studies inform larger pieces, photo references guide tonal decisions, and sketches test compositional choices before committing to final work. Through this portfolio I aim to demonstrate technical growth, visual curiosity, and the capability to translate observation into design ready assets for both print and digital contexts.
